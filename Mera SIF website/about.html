A note on Direct Plans and how we earn
As a SEBI-regulated AMFI Registered Distributor, Trustner deals exclusively in Regular Plans of mutual funds and SIFs. We do not promote, sell, or distribute Direct Plans. We are remunerated through trail commissions disclosed by the AMC — never directly by the investor. This is a standard distribution model in India, fully disclosed in every transaction confirmation. Investors who prefer Direct Plans should use AMC websites or SEBI Registered Investment Advisors (RIAs).

Why SIF matters to us strategically

The Specialized Investment Fund category is purpose-built for the exact client Trustner is structured to serve — the ₹10 lakh to ₹5 crore HNI looking for institutional-quality alternatives without the PMS overhead. Three reasons SIF became a strategic priority for our research desk:

1. The right wrapper for our core client

Our research and distribution architecture has always been focused on the emerging-affluent and HNI segments. Pre-SIF, this segment was structurally squeezed — too rich for retail MFs, too poor for PMS at the ₹50L floor. SIF closes that gap precisely.

2. The tax math is undeniable

For top-bracket investors, the ~3.2% post-tax IRR delta vs an equivalent AIF Cat-III translates to ₹70–80 lakh of preserved wealth per crore over a decade. No legitimate distributor can ignore that arithmetic when serving HNI clients.

3. Research credibility compounds

Wealth-management businesses that build research credibility in a category in its first 24 months own that conversation for the next decade. We've internalised the entire category — every live strategy, every fund manager pedigree, every regulatory development. Our advisors carry that depth into every client conversation.
