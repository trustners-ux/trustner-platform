How every edition is made

MeraSIF Monthly is compiled by the Trustner research desk from verifiable sources only — the same standard as everything on this site. If a figure can't be traced to a source, it doesn't get published.

Official AMFI NAVs
Every performance figure is computed from AMFI's published daily SIF NAVs (Regular-Growth plans) — the same engine behind our live Fund Universe.
Source-checked news
Every industry claim is checked against its original published source before it appears. Unverifiable claims are dropped, not softened.
Education, not advice
We are an AMFI-registered distributor. Editions explain the category and record the facts — they never make personalised buy or sell recommendations.
