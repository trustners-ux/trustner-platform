Apr 11, 2025
SEBI mandates standardised ISID disclosure format
SEBI Circular SEBI/HO/IMD/IMD-RAC/P/CIR/2025/54 mandates the standardised Investment Scheme Information Document (ISID) format for all SIF disclosures. Makes cross-fund comparison materially easier. Bi-monthly portfolio publication mandatory.
SEBI Circular
Apr 9, 2025
SEBI clarifies minimum threshold and employee carve-outs
SEBI Circular SEBI/HO/IMD/...CIR/2025/49 clarifies: passive market drift below the ₹10L floor doesn't trigger forced redemption. Active redemption that would breach the floor triggers mandatory full redemption. Employee carve-outs defined separately.
SEBI Circular
Apr 1, 2025
SIF framework goes LIVE
The Specialized Investment Fund category officially activates. Eligible AMCs can begin filing SIF schemes with SEBI — the start of India's newest SEBI-regulated investment category.
Framework Live
Feb 27, 2025
SEBI Master Circular for SIF
SEBI issues the foundational SIF Master Circular (SEBI/HO/IMD/IMD-PoD-1/P/CIR/2025/26). Defines the entire framework: AMC eligibility (two routes — Sound Track Record vs Alternate), seven permitted strategy categories, ₹10 lakh investor minimum, 25% unhedged short cap, exposure limits, disclosure norms.
SEBI Master CircularFoundational
Dec 16, 2024
SEBI amends MF Regulations — Chapter VI-C inserted
SEBI amends the SEBI (Mutual Funds) Regulations 1996 to insert Chapter VI-C, formally creating the SIF category. The amendment is the legal foundation for everything that follows. SIF inherits MF-grade tax treatment under Section 10(23D) precisely because of this amendment.
Regulation AmendmentFoundational
