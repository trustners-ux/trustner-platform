Is SIF Right For You?

7 questions. 60 seconds. Get your personalised SIF fit score plus a model allocation tailored to your corpus, risk profile, time horizon and tax bracket. No fluff. No sales pitch in the result.

Question 1 of 7
Question 1 of 7

What's your current liquid investment corpus?

Liquid means: equity, mutual funds, FD, debt MFs, PMS, AIF — excluding real estate and ULIPs.
Question 2 of 7

Your annual income (or HUF income)?

Determines your tax bracket — which determines how much SIF tax efficiency benefits you vs PMS/AIF.
Question 3 of 7

What's your investment time horizon?

SIFs need 3+ years to express their long-short alpha. Below that, you don't get the strategy benefit.
Question 4 of 7

How comfortable are you with portfolio drawdowns?

A drawdown is a temporary fall from peak. Equity-oriented SIFs can drop 20-30% in a real correction. Hybrids typically 5-10%.
Question 5 of 7

What do you want from this allocation?

Your primary objective drives which of the 7 SIF strategy categories fits you.
Question 6 of 7

Do you currently hold PMS or AIF Cat-III investments?

If yes, the SIF tax-bucket arbitrage is largest for you. Many investors are surprised by the size of the gap.
Question 7 of 7

Are you comfortable with limited liquidity?

SIFs may require 15 working days notice on redemption. Some are weekly or monthly liquidity (vs daily MF).
