## 1. Introduction

### What is MeraSIP?
MeraSIP (www.merasip.com) is Trustner's digital investment education and planning platform. It provides 30 free interactive financial calculators that help investors make informed decisions about SIPs, loans, taxes, insurance, and major life financial choices.

### Why Should You Use These Calculators?
- **Build Trust:** Show clients real numbers, not just promises
- **Visual Impact:** Every calculator has charts and graphs that make concepts easy to understand
- **PDF Reports:** Every calculator lets you download a PDF report that you can share with the client
- **Free to Use:** No login required, completely free for clients
- **Mobile-Friendly:** Works on phones, tablets, and computers
- **Real-Time:** All results update instantly as inputs change

### How This Guide is Organized
Each calculator is explained with:
- **What it does** (one-line summary)
- **When to use it** (client situations)
- **What the client enters** (input fields)
- **What the client sees** (results and charts)
- **Selling conversation tip** (how to convert interest into action)

## 2. How to Use the Calculators

### Accessing the Calculators
1. Go to **www.merasip.com/calculators**
2. You'll see all 30 calculators organized into 5 categories with quick-jump navigation pills at the top
3. Click any calculator card to open it

### Common Features (All 30 Calculators)
- **Left Panel:** Input fields where you enter numbers (sliders, buttons, or type directly)
- **Right Panel:** Results, charts, and tables update in real-time
- **Download PDF:** Every calculator has a "Download PDF" button to save a report

### Pro Tip for POSPs

## 11. Tips for POSPs & Sub-Brokers

### Do's
1. **Always use the client's real numbers** -- never use generic examples when you have their actual data
2. **Download PDFs** -- give the client a tangible takeaway from every meeting
3. **Start with a problem** -- use Insurance, Tax, or Emergency Fund calculators to identify gaps, then present SIP as the solution
4. **Show multiple scenarios** -- "What if you invest Rs 10K? Rs 15K? Rs 20K?"
5. **Use Cost of Delay in every meeting** -- it creates natural urgency without being pushy
6. **Bookmark your top 5 calculators** on your phone for instant access during meetings
7. **Follow the conversation flow**: Problem (gap identification) --> Solution (SIP/Insurance) --> Action (start today)

### Don'ts
1. **Don't promise exact returns** -- always say "assuming X% returns" and point to the disclaimer
2. **Don't skip the disclaimer** -- every calculator shows it; respect it
3. **Don't overwhelm** -- use 2-3 calculators per meeting, not all 30
4. **Don't use calculators as the ONLY sales tool** -- they support the conversation, not replace it
5. **Don't misrepresent** -- these are educational tools, not guarantees
