PRODUCT CREDIT RULES - DETAILED SUMMARY
================================================================================

MF SIP: 100% (full credit on annualized SIP amount)
MF Lumpsum Equity: 10% (low margin on one-time investments)
MF Lumpsum LT Debt: 7.5% (medium-term fixed income)
MF Lumpsum ST Debt: 5% (liquid/ultra-short funds)
MF Debt→Equity STP: 10% (conversion route)

Life Insurance:
  10 Years+ Regular: 125% (long-term customers)
  <10 Years Regular: 100% (standard long-term)
  ULIP Regular: 25% (lower margin variable products)
  Single Premium: 10% (low credit, one-time premium)

Health Insurance:
  Annual: 100% (standard)
  2-Year: Premium/2 * 125% (125% bonus for 2-year commitment)
  3-Year: Premium/3 * 150% (150% bonus for 3-year commitment)
  Quarterly/HY: 50% (discouraged)

GI Motor:
  Private Car (OD+TP): Grid-based per insurer (15-21%)
  CV (LCV/HCV): Grid-based per insurer (40-44%)
  Two Wheeler: Grid-based per insurer (15-18%)
  TP Only (PSU): Lower margin (8% typical), non-preferred

GI Non-Motor:
  Fire: 100%
  Marine: 100%
  Engineering: 100%
  Liability: 100%

GI GMC/GPA: 20% (corporate group, high volume, thin margin)
GI Renewal: 33% (one-third credit for retention)

POSP/Sub-broker:
  Self-Business: 300% (3x multiplier for RM's own sourced)
  POSP Normal: 30% (standard POSP channel)
  POSP Higher Pay: Proportionately less (adjusted for payout)
  Franchise: 15% (franchise business model)

Trail Sharing:
  Self-Sourced: RM 25%, Mgmt 15%, Co 60%
  Assigned + New: RM 15%, Mgmt 10%, Co 75%
  Assigned + No New: RM 0%, Mgmt 5%, Co 95%
  Office/Walk-in: RM 10%, Mgmt 10%, Co 80%

Accelerator Slabs:
  <80%: 0% rate (no incentive)
  80-100%: 4% at 1.0x (base)
  101-110%: 4% at 1.0x (standard)
  111-130%: 4.6% at 1.15x (enhanced)
  131-150%: 5% at 1.25x (super)
  150%+: 6% at 1.5x (champion)
